title: Salesforce
---

Salesforce is a cloud-based platform that provides customer relationship management (CRM) solutions. Businesses use Salesforce to streamline operations across sales, marketing, customer service, and more, helping to strengthen customer relationships.

By integrating Morph with Salesforce, users can easily create dashboards and reports leveraging Salesforce data.

## How to use

It can be created from the workspace by going Connections > Integrations.
