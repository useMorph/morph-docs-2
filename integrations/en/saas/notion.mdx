title: Notion
---

Notion is an all-in-one productivity tool that offers note-taking, task management, databases, and project management on a single platform. It helps users organize information and enhance collaboration, whether individually or within teams.

By integrating Morph with Notion, users can freely analyze and aggregate data stored in Notion databases and pages.

## How to use

It can be created from the workspace by going Connections > Integrations.
