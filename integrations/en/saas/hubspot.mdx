HubSpot is an all-in-one platform that integrates marketing, sales, and customer service, providing users with tools to strengthen customer relationships and drive business growth.

By integrating with Morph, users can create custom reports that cannot be generated directly within HubSpot. Additionally, by connecting with other data sources, it becomes possible to deliver deeper insights to the sales team through data applications.

## How to use

It can be created from the workspace by going Connections > Integrations.
