title: Freee
---

Freee is a cloud-based accounting software designed for small and medium-sized businesses in Japan. It helps users streamline their accounting tasks and gain real-time insight into their financial status. Additionally, Freee integrates with numerous financial institutions and services, providing automated data entry.

By integrating Morph with Freee, users can easily create dashboards and reports that leverage data from Freee.

## How to use

It can be created from the workspace Connections > Integrations.
