---
title: Intercom
---

Intercom is a messaging platform designed to streamline communication with customers. Users can provide real-time chat and email support directly within websites and apps. Additionally, Intercom includes customer data management and analytics features, supporting marketing and sales efforts.

By integrating Morph with Intercom, users can easily create dashboards and reports that leverage data from Intercom.

## How to use

It can be created from the workspace by going Connections > Integrations.
